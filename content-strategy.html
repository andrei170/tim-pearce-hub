Phase 3

Content Strategy - Dr Tim Pearce

Prepared: 2026-05-17 Distribution doc. How the existing Instagram, YouTube, and free-resources surfaces route into the scorecard and the cohort funnel WITHOUT adding any new content load on Tim.

The Premise

Tim is already publishing at a cadence the funnel cannot currently absorb. ~382K Instagram followers, the #1 aesthetics YouTube show with 8 million cumulative views, a press surface across Independent + Newsweek + The Sun + Daily Mail + Insider + New York Post + Aesthetics magazine, and a free resources library covering Complications + Techniques + Consultation + Lips + Longevity + Business + All Downloads. The bottleneck is not "more content." The bottleneck is the routing layer between every existing piece of content and the buy surface.

This strategy adds zero new content production to Tim's plate. It re-routes the existing content into the four-piece funnel installed underneath.

1. Instagram (Primary Acquisition Surface, ~382K Followers)

Bio Link Routing

Current state: bio link routes to drtimpearce.com homepage.

Post-install state: bio link routes to the scorecard, with the homepage as a secondary route accessible via the scorecard hub or via a Linktree-style secondary surface.

Why: every cold viewer who clicks the bio link from a reel currently lands on the catalogue and has to self-select a tier. The scorecard takes 90 seconds, captures email at the moment of capture, and returns a routed recommendation. The catalogue still gets visited - it just gets visited by a router-tagged visitor instead of a cold self-selecter.

Reel Caption CTAs

Current state: reel captions point to bio link or to a specific course.

Post-install state: reel captions point to the scorecard for any reel that crosses three or more procedural categories or that addresses a readiness-band question. Reels that are tightly scoped to a single technique still point to the specific course buy page (with the VSL above it).

Caption template for cross-category reels:

Not sure where you are on the injector ladder? 90-second scorecard tells you which course tier is the right next step. Link in bio.

Story Cadence

Current state: stories cycle through clinic moments, before-and-after stills (where consented), course promotions, and the Instagram-only Art Codes discount.

Post-install state: weekly story slot pinned to the scorecard. Monthly story slot pinned to the cohort application landing. Course promotion stories continue as today.

Pinned Reels Audit

Current state: pinned reels cycle through technique demonstrations and the Free Full Face Filler Web Class promotion.

Post-install state: pin one reel that walks the scorecard surface (filmed on a phone, 30 seconds, "here is the 90-second scorecard, here is what it returns, here is what to do with the readout"). Pin one reel that walks the cohort surface (filmed on a phone, 45 seconds, "the cohort is the operator-development step above the asynchronous courses, here is who it is for, here is who it is not for, here is how the application works").

2. YouTube (Long-Form Authority Surface, #1 Aesthetics Show, 8M Cumulative Views)

Description Box Routing

Current state: description box links to the course shop.

Post-install state: description box links to the scorecard as the primary route, with the course shop as the secondary route.

Description template:

Not sure which course tier is the right next step? 90-second scorecard returns a readiness-band readout and a one-click route to the matched course. [Scorecard URL]

Full course catalogue: [drtimpearce.com URL]

End-Screen Routing

Current state: end-screen cards cycle through related videos and the course shop.

Post-install state: end-screen retains one "next video" card and one "scorecard" card. Course shop link drops to the description box only.

Video Topic Selection

No change required. The existing topic mix (technique demonstrations, complications walk-throughs, consultation framing, clinic-builder content) maps directly to the four readiness bands the scorecard returns. The topic mix is the funnel.

Pinned Comment

Pinned comment on every flagship video:

90-second scorecard returns the right course tier for where you inject today: [Scorecard URL]

3. Free Resources Library

Current state: drtimpearce.com/free-resources hosts the Complications + Techniques + Consultation + Lips + Longevity + Business + All Downloads clusters plus the Free Full Face Filler Web Class.

Post-install state: every PDF download triggers the Free Web Class opt-in welcome sequence (see email-sequence.md, Sequence A). The Web Class opt-in routes the viewer into the readiness-band scorecard at Day 7.

Sidebar on every resource cluster page: persistent scorecard CTA card.

4. Trustpilot

Current state: Trustpilot review surface at trustpilot.com/review/drtimpearce.com.

Post-install state: Trustpilot strip embedded on the drtimpearce.com homepage hero (below the VSL) and on the cohort landing. No new content from Tim; the Trustpilot widget pulls live.

5. Press Surface

Current state: Independent, Newsweek, The Sun, Daily Mail, Insider, New York Post, Scottish Hair and Beauty Awards 2023, Aesthetics magazine logos cited on the brand.

Post-install state: press logo strip carried across to the hub, the cohort landing, the scorecard surface (above the question flow), and the VSL opener slate. Logos are the cold-trust anchor.

7. The "No New Content From Tim" Rule

Every piece of routing above re-uses content Tim already publishes. The VSL is one recording day. The scorecard readout is one approval pass on copy AG1 drafts. The cohort landing is one approval pass on copy AG1 drafts. The welcome sequences are one approval pass each. After day-7 kickoff, the funnel runs forever on Tim's existing publishing cadence.

If Tim wants to add any new content for the cohort specifically:

  • One 60-second cohort intro reel (filmed at the desk, no production).
  • One 5-minute cohort walk-through video (filmed at the desk, embedded on the cohort landing).
  • One office-hours session per month inside the Profinity Academy / cohort channel.

That is the maximum additive load. The funnel works on day 7 even if Tim adds none of the above.
