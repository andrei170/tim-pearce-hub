What This Is Not

  • This is not a course build. The Foundation, Complications, Filler Complications, Lip Design, and Longevity courses exist and stay as the curriculum surfaces.
  • This is not an advisory engagement. The cohort is practitioner development and clinic-builder scaffolding, not "make money with aesthetics."
  • This is not a re-platform. drtimpearce.com stays. The eLearning catalogue stays. The free resources surface stays.
  • This is not a brand pivot. The operator-educator register on Tim's IG and YouTube stays. The AG1-built surfaces sit underneath in voice-matched register, complementing the brand, not replacing it.

Risk Register

  • Regulatory tone. Every cohort, scorecard, landing, and welcome-flow surface stays at the level of practitioner education and operator development. No income claims for the injector. No patient-side procedural promise. No advertising of prescription-only medicine to a consumer audience. Footer disclaimer present on every public surface.
  • Voice register separation. AG1-built surfaces stay in voice-matched register to Tim's existing channel voice. The AG1 pitch surfaces (DM, LinkedIn, email) stay in AG1's operator-grounded register. They do not attempt to mimic Tim's clinical-educator voice. The pitch is operator-to-operator about the FUNNEL.
  • No fabricated tech. Every gap listed is observable from drtimpearce.com, the IG bio surface, the YouTube show, the trustpilot.com review surface, and the public testimonial wall.
  • Brand-colour respect. AG1 default colours (gold accent + black) on hub and landing. Tim's dark navy + teal accent palette referenced for the install only. Brand colours and copy lock with Tim on day one of the install.
