The Four Pieces

Four installs. Sit on top of the existing brand.

No course rebuild. No re-platform. No brand pivot. The four pieces patch the gaps between the audience, the buy decision, and the warm trained-injector base.

Piece 01 · Highest-Leverage Pre-Sell
01

The 5-7 minute VSL on drtimpearce.com hero + above the four premium course buy pages.

The buy decision currently happens on a flat course-card grid. No long-form video pre-sell above the homepage hero. No long-form video pre-sell above any per-course buy page. ~382K Instagram followers plus the #1 aesthetics YouTube show convert (or do not) on a card grid that could belong to any eLearning storefront. The fix is a 5-7 minute VSL filmed in Tim's operator-educator register, embedded on drtimpearce.com hero plus above the BOTOX Foundation, Botulinum Toxin Complications, Dermal Filler Complications, and Lip Design buy pages.

We build the 5-7 minute VSL that pre-sells the right course tier for the right practitioner band. Script, direct the record, edit the cut, embed on all five surfaces, set captions. The voice on screen is Tim's.

Compounds against every Instagram bio click, every YouTube description link, every Trustpilot route-in, and every cold visitor arriving from the press surface.

Piece 02 · The Qualification Step
02

The 90-second readiness scorecard between the audience and the buy.

Three different practitioner bands land on the same flat catalogue: the new-to-injectables medical professional, the intermediate injector building case load, and the senior injector building a clinic. The shop does not ask "where are you in your injector journey" and does not route to a tier matched to the answer. The fix is a 10-12 question scorecard that returns Too Early / Foundation-Ready / Complications-Ready / Cohort-Ready, plus a one-click route to the matched buy surface (or to the cohort application if the readiness band lands in the top tier). Captures email. Tags by readiness band, by procedural goal, and by clinic surface.

We install the "Are You Injection-Ready For This Course Tier?" scorecard that filters before checkout. Question logic, scoring algorithm, routing rules, readout copy, email tagging. The recommendations route through Tim's ladder.

Filters fit-mismatched buyers out of the wrong tier, lifts review quality downstream, captures email at scale from ~382K IG plus the YouTube audience for the first time.

Piece 03 · The Application-Gated Cohort
03

The Aesthetic Practitioner Graduate Cohort - the unfilled slot above the asynchronous catalogue.

The ladder tops out at the asynchronous Dermal Filler Complications curriculum. The 23,927+ trained-injector base plus the existing Profinity Academy reference plus the WhatsApp practitioner cohorts cited in the testimonial wall are the warmest possible list for a tier above the asynchronous catalogue and currently have no publicly routed funnel into it. Format: 8-12 verified practitioners per cycle, application-gated via Typeform, 8-week cohort with weekly live group calls in Tim's register, two office-hours blocks per month, and a "graduate clinical scaffolding" sign-off at the end. The application is the filter. The cap is the moat.

We build the application and landing for the Graduate Cohort. Cohort landing copy, Typeform application, scorecard-to-cohort routing, post-application email sequence, Profinity Academy bridge. Tim runs the cohort.

Respects the regulatory tone. Positioned as practitioner development at the senior end, not patient-side outcome promise. The peer room is the differentiator from every asynchronous tier in the existing ladder.

Piece 04 · The Segmented Welcome Flow
04

The buyer-tier segmentation underneath the existing course buyer base.

The Foundation Course buyer, the Toxin Complications buyer, the Filler Complications buyer, the Lip Design buyer, and the Free Web Class opt-in all route into the same generic post-purchase email. The Filler Complications buyer who paid four figures gets the same onboarding nurture as the Foundation Course buyer who paid low three figures. The fix is an eLearning webhook handler that tags by product on the purchase event and forks the welcome sequence per tier. Day 7 cross-sells route per tier. Day 30 the Filler Complications buyer gets a graduate-cohort application invite the Foundation buyer is not yet ready for.

We wire the segmented welcome flow that tags Foundation, Toxin Complications, Filler Complications, Lip Design, and Free Web Class opt-ins into different downstream cadences. eLearning webhook handler, email-system tags, welcome templates per tier, content-origin tagging for the Free Resources opt-ins.

Captures the Filler-Complications-to-cohort upgrade path the existing buyer base does not currently have. Builds the warmest possible audience for any future cohort cycle.

A Note On Tone

Regulated-medical register, every surface.

Aesthetic medicine eLearning sits inside an actively regulated environment in the UK (GMC, JCCP, CQC, ASA / CAP) and in the US (FDA plus state-level medical board scope). Every public surface in this package stays at the level of practitioner education and operator development. No income claims for the injector. No patient-side outcome promises framed as guarantees. No advertising of prescription-only medicine to a consumer audience. The cohort sells practitioner development, not patient-side promise. The scorecard filters fit-mismatched buyers before checkout, which lifts review quality at the source. The AG1-built surfaces sit underneath Tim's existing audience-facing register, complementing the brand, not replacing it.

Regulated-Medical Disclaimer

Educational content only. Not regulated medical advice. Aesthetic medical procedures carry inherent risks; outcomes vary. Consult a qualified medical professional in your jurisdiction. The scorecard, the courses, and the cohort are educational. Nothing in this hub is a patient-side recommendation or an income guarantee.
